About Us

We mainly cook, package and deliver healthy food in parcels to those who need it most. Many children homes, orphanages, homeless shelters, and some schools rely on us to provide them with meals

This organization started in the year 2014, 1 August and we have been running for 10 years strong

We recognise the importance of enriches people's lives and the simplest way is through their stomachs. Over 50% of the South African population is below the poverty line and cannot afford everyday expenses

Our Mission

The purpose of this particular organization is to alleviate hunger and nourish many communities by providing nutritious, cooked meals to these individuals and families. We are committed to fostering a sense of belonging for all, and ensuring that no one ever goes to bed hungry.
